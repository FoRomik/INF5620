News

  • Sep 16: From now on you need to send in files through a project hosting site, see note about delivery of exercises and projects.

  • Sep 11: Corrected large amounts of typos in the course notes.
  • Sep 9: Added a plan for the course.

Homework

Sep 13 and 20

Exercises 24, 25, 26. Anyone who hands in these before the presentation of the solution on Thursday, Sep 13, will get 1 credit point per exercise. A student group (Yapi and friends) will present the solution.

Exercise 27 can also be solved. Note that you have to hand in exercises through Bitbucket or GitHub, see delivery of exercises and projects.

Exercise 15-17 will be presented on Sep 20 (by Nina and friends).
